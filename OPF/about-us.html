Who We Are

At Old People Fun, we’re dedicated to making retirement the best time of your life. Our mission is simple: to provide retirees with exciting, affordable, and enriching travel experiences. We understand that life after retirement is a new beginning—a chance to explore, learn, and have fun in ways you never imagined.

Our Story

Founded by travel enthusiasts with a passion for adventure and a love for solving puzzles, Old People Fun was born out of the desire to combine travel with a bit of friendly competition and exploration. We believe that the best vacations are those that not only show you the world but also engage your mind and spirit.

Our Values

  • Adventure: We believe that life is an adventure at any age.
  • Affordability: You’ve worked hard your whole life. Now it’s time to enjoy it, without breaking the bank.
  • Engagement: Our scavenger hunts are designed to stimulate both body and mind.
  • Community: Traveling is better when shared. Whether you’re traveling solo or with friends, you’ll meet other fun-loving retirees on our trips.
