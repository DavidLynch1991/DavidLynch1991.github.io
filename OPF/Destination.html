Destinations Around the Globe!

At Old People Fun, we bring you a world of possibilities. Explore carefully curated destinations, each handpicked for their beauty, history, and accessibility. Our trips are designed to provide comfort and ease while still delivering the thrill of discovery. Whether you're seeking peaceful retreats or exciting cultural experiences, we've got something for every retiree.

Featured Destinations:

Paris, France

Wander the streets of the romantic city of lights, see the Eiffel Tower, and discover hidden corners with a twist of a scavenger hunt.

Kyoto, Japan

Immerse yourself in traditional tea ceremonies, explore serene gardens, and uncover the secrets of ancient temples.

Machu Picchu, Peru

A bucket-list destination for many, now accessible with expert guidance and a treasure hunt to uncover its ancient mysteries.

Santorini, Greece

Take in stunning sunsets, enjoy delicious Mediterranean cuisine, and engage in a scavenger hunt to find the island’s best-kept secrets.

New Orleans, USA

Dive into the vibrant culture of the French Quarter, enjoy soulful jazz, and unravel the hidden stories of the city’s past.

Each of our destinations features a custom scavenger hunt designed to make your journey more exciting. Collect clues, solve puzzles, and explore landmarks in a unique way, making every trip not just a vacation but an adventure!
